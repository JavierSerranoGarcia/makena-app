Makena – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 May 2025

Makena is a body shape and style recommendation app. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and what is stored.

Data we do not collect

Makena does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account or registration is required.
  • No names, email addresses, or contact details are collected.
  • No usage analytics or crash reporting data is sent to any server.
  • No advertising identifiers are accessed or shared.

Camera

Makena requests access to your device camera solely to perform on-device body pose detection. When you initiate a scan:

  • A single photo is captured and passed immediately to the on-device ML model.
  • The photo is never saved to your camera roll, stored on disk, or transmitted over the network.
  • The image is discarded as soon as the measurements are extracted.
  • All processing happens entirely on your device.

Measurements and results

Any measurements or style results generated by the app exist only in memory during your session. They are not written to permanent storage and are not shared with any third party.

Third-party services

Makena uses Google ML Kit for pose detection. ML Kit runs entirely on-device and does not send image data to Google servers. See Google ML Kit Terms of Service for further details.

Children

Makena does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Because no data is collected from any user, the app is safe for all ages.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a future version of the app, the updated policy will be published at this URL with a revised date.
